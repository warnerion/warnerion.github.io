About Me

I received B.S. degree in automation science and engineering from East China University of Science and Technology, China, in 2015 and M.S. degree in control theory and engineering from Tongji University, China, in 2018. I am currently a Ph.D student in computer engineering in the University of California, Santa Cruz advised by Prof. Sanfelice.

Research
